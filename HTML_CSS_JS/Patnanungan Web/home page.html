Patnanungan Island is the natural workshop of evolution!
A shoe-shaped island facing the philipinne sea.

Reason why our island is a place to visit!

The Talisman Town Of Patnanungan Is Located In The East; Life Is Simple Here, But The Environment Is Peaceful And Rich. A gentle southwesterly breeze blows, and the sand sparkles like a pearl. You Will Be Astounded By The Beautiful Scenery When You Get Here. The people of Patnanungan are friendly, hardworking, and welcoming, and the island is full of joy and happiness. Crab, fish, and shellfish are abundant in the sea here. You will be amused on our island's farm, where green rice is harvested.
